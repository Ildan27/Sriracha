OUR STORY

The journey of bringing authentic Asian flavors to your kitchen

The Beginning

Our story begins in the coastal town of Si Racha, Thailand, where our founder David Tran first developed his passion for bold flavors and the perfect chili sauce. Growing up in a family where meals were a central part of daily life, David learned the art of balancing flavors from his grandmother, who would make homemade chili sauces using sun-ripened chilies from their garden.

In 1975, David brought his family recipes to America, carrying with him little more than a dream and his grandmother's traditional methods for creating the perfect chili sauce. Starting in a small kitchen in Los Angeles, he began crafting small batches of his signature sauce, delivering them personally to local Asian restaurants and markets in repurposed baby food jars.

Growing the Brand

What began as a small family operation quickly gained popularity through word of mouth. Chefs across California began requesting the sauce for their restaurants, drawn to its perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and garlic notes. By the early 1980s, we had moved from David's kitchen to our first small factory.

Our iconic rooster logo and green-capped bottles became recognized symbols of quality and flavor. We remained committed to small-batch production methods while expanding our reach, ensuring that every bottle maintained the authentic taste that had earned us our reputation.

Throughout the 1990s and early 2000s, what had started as a specialized condiment in Asian markets grew into a mainstream culinary phenomenon. Today, our sauces can be found in homes, restaurants, and food establishments worldwide, having transcended cultural boundaries to become a global flavor sensation.
